Hybrid Hire

Hire by the hour, for the day, or lock out a few weeks to work with our team in the creative heart of Lagos, Nigeria .

Accommodating all kinds of studio needs, since opening 5 years ago, we have become a go-to place for professional writing sessions and have proudly hosted talented producers and artists including - Mr Hudson, Laura Mvula, Tiana Major9, Nafe Smallz, P2J, Biig Piig, JD Reid, Sly, Daughter, Kojey Radical, Jvck James, Tion Wayne, Jords and many more.

Our studios are also often used for film or photography projects. The modern Scandinavian-influenced designs and unique aesthetics make the perfect backdrop for any visual content-creation.

What to Expect

Regardless of what service you choose, here’s what you can expect when you choose Arrow House.

Amazing Spaces

Each studio is built with the utmost attention to detail. Our bespoke architectural acoustics and aesthetics provide serene creative environments for all forms of music-making.

Excellent Equipment

Our studios are packed with the very best recording equipment available. We have gorgeous vintage mics, a world-class signal chain for vocals, as well as high-quality outboard gear and an extensive library of plug-ins. View the full Noatune Equipment List here.

Friendly & Skilled Team

Upon arrival at Arrow House, will ensure each session starts smoothly. When working with our team, we guarantee each and every client has the most productive and unique sessions possible.

Customisable Atmosphere

All of our studios are equipped with Philips Hue Smart Lights and preset moods. Whether it’s to help kick-start your creativity, or provide a beautiful backdrop to photos & videos, these add the perfect vibe for every setting.

For Any Occasion

Whilst they’re all equipped for just about all modern music production needs, each of our studios come with their own distinct flavour and set up. Take a look at our studio pages to see which studio best suits your specific needs.

Perfect Location

A short walk from London Fields Overground and Bethnal Green Underground - Noatune can be found in the creative hub of Hackney, London. Countless restaurants, bars and cafés are right outside and London Fields park nearby for a perfect lunch break.
