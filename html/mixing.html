Mixing

We offer professional mixing services by engineers who take the time to understand the client's vision, artistry and budget. All of our mixing is bespoke and quoted according to each project. With accurate monitoring and legendary digital outboard gear, Arrow House mixing services give you the competitive edge and the sound your music needs to excite listeners' ears.

We have worked on Mixes for artists such as Mizzle, Merry-Lynn and mixed for brands including Soundpool and Taepsstudios.

Digital Mixing interface

Our full digital mixing outboard combines the workfloor advantages of Cubase tools and high-end plugins with sought-after digital mixing equipment to give your mixes some depth and character. We all love our plugins – but wait until you hear our vocals through a real Teletronix LA-2A or Tube-Tech CL 1B.
