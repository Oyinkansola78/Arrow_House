Additional Mixing Services

We offer specialist services to cover all of your mixing needs.

Editing, vocal tuning and post-production

In addition to full mixing services, we offer specialised services upon request, including vocal editing and tuning as well as vocal alignment to pre-existing stereo tracks. This vocal-refinement service can transform ordinary vocals into a great sounding performance, match recorded vocals to tracks or beats pre-recorded at different tempos, or align dialog for video post-production.

Vocal alignment of backing vocals

Audio restoration for poor recordings eg. Noise reduction, declipping

Sonically match recordings done in multiple places

Tidying up drum and live band recordings in a musical way

We focus on the mixing, let us do the ‘boring’ stuff

Mix-Aid/Part Mix/Stem Enhancement

A lot of us are creating and mixing great music from home these days without access to high-end studio equipment. With this service, our engineers will run your recordings or selected stems through our analog gear to transform plain sounding recordings to compelling sounds to reintroduce to your own mixes. This is the perfect way to improve your existing recordings and retain full control over the mix with the benefit of higher quality audio to work with.

Perfect for producing and mixing without access to high end microphones, outboard gear or amps.

Improve vocal recordings by running them through a Warm WA 273 Preamp/Eq, a Midas 32 live compressor or anything else on our gear list.

Match recordings done in various spaces

This service is charged per hour or quote and you can attend the sessions or get it done remotely.
