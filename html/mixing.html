Mixing

We offer professional mixing services by engineers who take the time to understand the client's vision, artistry and budget. All of our mixing is bespoke and quoted according to each project. With accurate monitoring and legendary analogue outboard gear, Noatune mixing services give you the competitive edge and the sound your music needs to excite listeners' ears.

We have worked on mixes for Biig Piig, Nafe Smallz, D Double E, Laidback Luke, Bryde, Aeris Roves and have mixed for labels and brands including Sony Music, Universal Music, IKEA and Warner Music.

The Best of Both Worlds

Porque no los dos? Our full hybrid mixing service combines the workflow advantages of Pro Tools and high-end plugins with sought-after analog mixing equipment to give your mixes some depth and character. We all love our plugins - but wait until you hear your vocals through a real Teletronix LA-2A or Tube-Tech CL 1B with classic effects like the Fulltone Tube Tape Echo.
