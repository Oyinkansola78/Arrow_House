Studio 3

Our producer’s suite.

Studio C is perfect for anyone primarily working on electronic music production, beat-making, mixing and cutting amazing vocals in the same space. Studio C is also perfect for finishing mixes with an industry-standard monitoring system and a fully-loaded iMac with the most popular high-end plug-ins.

The studio is prepped for a hassle-free session set up with a Neumann U87 and Neve 1073 Preamp running straight into our Universal Apollo Interface, with the choice of connecting to your laptop or our top-spec iMac. You will have the ability to quickly produce demos that can easily be turned into record-quality productions.

Studio C is our most affordable room and is equipped for those who may only want the basics but need them to be of the highest standard.
