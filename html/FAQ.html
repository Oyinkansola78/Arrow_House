FAQs

At Arrow house Studios we have experienced in-house staff comprising of engineers and assistants. For more information on recording with an engineer, to enquire about a recording session or to get a quote, kindly contact us.

We’re open 24/7 for bookings online, or you can book a tour to view the studio and book you in for the same day.

We don’t hire out empty studios for exclusive long-term use. If you have a specific project in mind, contact us for details of package deals. For long-term studio sessions, check out our monthly bundles for hugely discounted sessions and regular studio access.

Absolutely. Many producers/composers/songwriters hire our studios and work from their laptops. Midas M 32 R live are set up in all three studios.

Yes, all our studios are soundproofed and have carefully designed room acoustics.

Yes, when you book several consecutive days. If you are considering recording your next EP or Album or if you need a number of songs mixed- kindly enquire for a quote.

Cancellations of confirmed bookings made up to 72hours prior to booking commencing will be subject to a cancellation fee of 25 – 50% of the session total. The fee will be at Arrow House Studios discretion and factor in studio demand and any lost bookings as a result of this booking being taken/being cancelled. Where possible, we will try and accommodate a reschedule.

Yes, there are few dedicated parking at Arrow House Studios. However, if they are taken, you can park on the streets around the Studio.

Yes, but we have to be informed before you bring two or more guests. The hirer must be present at all times if you invite external musicians/guests.

All studios have great collection of amazing plugins such as Waves.

Yes, drum recordings take place in Studio 1 where you can record professional live drums on your tracks

Yes, we have dimmable lights and Philips Hue lighting in all studios so you can create an atmosphere that makes you creative and comfortable.

Yes, all our studios have air-conditioning as well as quiet ventilation systems that bring in fresh filtered air from outside to make sessions as comfortable as possible.

Absolutely ! You will find a TRS patch bay in each studio and various adaptor cables to connect your own gear to Arrow House Studios Set up.

We do on occasion hire trainees to join our engineers during sessions – so feel free to send us your CV along with some songs you have worked on and specify what you did on them. We may not have time to respond to all queries like this – but we will read them and keep them on a file.
