Podcast / Voiceover

ADR / Voiceover

Not just for music recording, Studio A and Studio B is also purpose-built for video post-production. Both studios features a dedicated vocal booth with monitor screen and a view of the main room. Directors or engineers can sit comfortably and listen to ADR, as well as watch the proceedings in real-time on a large screen and provide feedback and direction to the voice actor. We are very experienced with remote work and happy to implement SourceConnect, ListenTo, CleanFeed and video via Zoom if not everyone in your team can attend the studios.
