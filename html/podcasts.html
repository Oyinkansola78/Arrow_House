Podcast / Voiceover

Our high-end studios located in Anthony have the perfect settings for all of your voice recording needs. Accommodating up to six guests or more in a relaxed sofa settings or round table format, Studio 1 & Studio 2 are customisable for different sets for any recording situation.

We have multiple smart mood lights, Presonus HP 60 head amp, EV RE20 microphones, SHURE SRH440-E Headphones and Shure SM7B microphones. These are often paired with an UAD 4-710D twin finity preamp or other amazing preamps for a rich clean sound. Check out the full gear list here.

Voiceover & ADR

Audiobook Recording

Our vocal recording expertise and studio setup for podcasting and video post production also makes Arrow House Studios a great choice for recording audiobooks.

ADR / Voiceover

Not just for music recording, Studio 3 and Studio 4 is also a purpose-built for video post-production. Both studios features a dedicated vocal booth with monitor screen and a view of the main room. Directors or engineers can sit comfortably and listen to ADR, as well as watch the proceedings in real-time through a large screen and provide feedback and direction to the voice over actor. We are very experienced with remote work and happy to implement SouceConnect, ListenTo, CleanFeed and video via Zoom if not everyone in your team can attend the studios.
