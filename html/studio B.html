Studio 2

An impeccable vocal tracking experience.

Studio 2 offers a comfortable and intimate creative space with rustic hardwood floors and beautiful wooden finishing. Designed with our most trusted artists and clients’ workflow in mind, musicians and producers can move away from the screen to work out ideas and get inspired.

Studio 2 has a window to let the natural light in and connect you to the vibrant surroundings of Lagos. It’s the perfect space for mix engineers, producers or musicians looking for an all-in-one creative space. If you are looking for an undisturbed, warm and inviting environment to create in, then you will love studio 2.
