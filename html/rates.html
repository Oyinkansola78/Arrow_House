Our Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

Cancellations of confirmed bookings made up to 72hours prior to booking commencing will be subject to a cancellation fee of 25 – 50% of the session total. The fee will be at Arrow House Studios discretion and factor in studio demand and any lost bookings as a result of this booking being taken/being cancelled. Where possible, reschedule at least 24hrs before your session commences as this is not subject to any fee.
