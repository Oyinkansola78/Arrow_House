Our Cancellation & Booking Policy

Cancellations of confirmed bookings made up to 72hours prior to booking commencing will be subject to a cancellation fee of 25 – 50% of the session total. The fee will be at Arrow House Studios discretion and factor in studio demand and any lost bookings as a result of this booking being taken/being cancelled. Where possible, we will try and accommodate a reschedule. Note that we only accept transfers and card payment. Ensure you arrive at least 15 minutes before your session starts
