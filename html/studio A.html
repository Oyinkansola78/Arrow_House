Studio 1

Our ultimate writing & recording studio.

This is our largest studio and features a spacious recording/control room with a separate isolation booth for perfect vocal tracking. The acoustics have been custom designed for well-rounded, lively recordings while ensuring that the studio also provides accurate monitoring. Studio 1 is the ultimate setting for larger writing and production sessions with space enough for guests.

With an incredible collection of preamps, analogue mixing gear and an impressive mic collection, working in Studio 1 is a dream. The space is suitable for band recordings with live drums, small orchestral setups, podcasts as well as live video performance.
